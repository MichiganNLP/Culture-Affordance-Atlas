Human Universal 3: Cooking

Drawing on Brown’s universal around “cooking” and food preparation, our “Cooking” category encompasses vessels, utensils, and engines of heat that transform raw ingredients into meals. From clay stoves to modern gas burners, cooking implements are foundational across every society. “Cooking” was one of our first-edition affordances in CAA to showcase this essential, cross-cultural function. The following are corresponding affordances mapped from Dollar Street.

gathering and storing drinking water

providing heat for cooking

providing space for eating

holding food during cooking

storing perishable food

serving and eating food

preparing food ingredients
